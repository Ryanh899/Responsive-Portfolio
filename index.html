About Me

I am a Software Engineer. I was born on October 5, 1999 in Laguna Niguel, California. I live and have lived in San Clemente, California my whole life. I attended Capistrano Valley Christian Schools and graduated in 2018. While at Capistrano Valley Christian Schools I worked at a local restaurant named Café Del Sol as a cashier/server. I also worked as a professional portrait photographer, often for senior photos and family portraits.

After High School I attended Saddleback College with the intentions of transferring to a four-year university and attaining a degree in business with an emphasis in finance. While at Saddleback College I was still working at Café Del Sol as well as running my photography business. I branched out my professional photography to the surf world and started shooting as an independent contractor for Hobie Surfboards. Now I am studying Software Engineering at UCI as part of a full stack coding boot-camp.
